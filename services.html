RESIDENTIAL - COMMERCIAL

Design

Looking for the best landscape designers in the area? Look no further than Enivroscape. Since 1981, we have used our expertise to take the ordinary and transform it into an exquisite piece of landscape art. Our experienced team of designers evaluates each individual landscape on several different levels before suggesting a plan of action. At Enviroscape, we listen to what each individual client envisions as part of their landscape and translate their ideas into a comprehensive landscaping plan. Beginning with the end in mind, each of our landscape designs is also created to look fantastic today and fabulous 10 years from now. So, whether it be a simple planning sketch to convey ideas, or a full scale landscape master plan, Enviroscape is prepared to get things started.

 

 

Landscape installation

Each of our landscape installation teams are lead by fully trained and accredited team leaders who are knowledgeable in the landscape construction and proper horticulture practices and techniques. Every detail is planned when properly installing a quality landscape to ensure a healthy outdoor living environment that can be enjoyed for years, if not generations to come.

 

Hardscapes

The term "landscaping" has evolved over the years and any more, a landscape project almost always involves some sort of "Hardscape" element. Enviroscape does the job right. With fully trained craftsmen on our staff, we make sure that just as much effort goes into the areas that you can't see, like base preparation and foundation work, as the finished details like cuts and proper joinery. Enviroscape is an authorized contractor for Belgard and Unilock products and can utilize many other options such as natural stone, pavers, segmental retaining walls, and more.

Maintenance

Quality landscaping requires a certain amount of care and upkeep. The trees, shrubs, and flowers in your landscape are living things that require proper care to grow and thrive. Your hardscape materials are subjected to extreme elements such as temperature and other weather conditions. Enviroscape has the expert staff to properly care for you lawn, landscape, and exterior environment in a professional yet affordable manner. Our team has been providing landscape maintenance services for residential and commercial properties since 1981. Our sound management practices ensure that your investment is well cared for.

 

As a member of the Indiana Nursery and Landscape association (INLA) and the  Professional Lawn-care Network (PLANET), Enviroscape keeps its employees educated and trained in the latest lawn-care and horticultural techniques as well as any required certifications that might be required.
